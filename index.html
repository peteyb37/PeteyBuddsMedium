Who Am I?

Aspiring Musician

I am an early 20's musician and software engineer currently going to college at Rochester Institute of Technology in New York. I write musio that follows a tone of soft/indie rock and acoustic memory. I do this for fun and for enjoyment for both myself and others and want to share what I've done with the world! Follow me on Soundcloud for latest updates. Thank you.

Currently working on an album that will be available first on Spotify

Inspiration

The World Gives Perspective

Each day, my life follows a routine. Whether it be work or school, I always wake up with a positive outlook, regardless of the day before. Each day, I go out into the world and I look for something I missed in previous days. It could be the blue tint in the sky or a happy family walking in the park. There's a lot to see if we look.

What we see is not just what we see. What we see is a story and these stories are everywhere. Most often, we can see love if we look. I try to write my songs about my feelings and my experiences, either personal or from what I've seen out in the world.

How You Can Help

People

Take some time out of your day and listen to some of my music. Let it inspire you to be yourself or to support others in their lives. A little effort can go a long way.

Every day, people struggle. Whatever it may be that causes this, we can be the answer for many of those people. Take the opportunities given to you to give back to others.
